Crunchy Chocolate Bites · Each bag contains 2 servings of crunchy bite-sized pieces of protein covered in rich & creamy chocolate. Each delicious bite contains a combination of natural superfoods to keep you mentally alert and promote strength & endurance.

Nutrition Facts

Roasted Cacao Beans

We handmake our High Performance chocolate using gourmet Arriba Nacional cacao beans to get its rich flavor and high antioxidant value.

MCT Oil

This flavor contains medium-chain triglycerides — fatty acids from coconuts that your liver converts into sustained energy. Our MCT oil starts as cold-pressed virgin coconut oil that undergoes a steam distillation process - no chemicals involved.

Maca

Maca is a highly nutritious superfood from Peru with tremendous antioxidant activity. It provides these protein bites with a mildly sweet butterscotch flavor.

Beetroot

Beetroot, aka beets, is one of our special ingredients for this flavor. They're high in dietary nitrates - compounds with beneficial vascular effects like reducing blood pressure, increasing blood flow and enhancing exercise performance, and muscle contractions.

Guarana

Whole guarana seeds are grinded into our High Performance Chocolate to provide you with helpful catabolic antioxidants like theobromine, tannins, saponins and catechins.

 

GRIT contains only natural ingredients and never chemical preservatives or artificial ingredients of any kind.

Lemon Cookie Bites · Each bag contains 2 servings of crunchy bite-sized pieces of protein covered in a layer of sweet & tangy lemon chocolate. That's our handmade white chocolate with added collagen protein (from Atlantic Cod), organic maca, turmeric root and cold-pressed lemon oil for a tasty way to support your healthy skin, hair, joints and digestive system.

Nutrition Facts

Lemon Oil

We use cold pressed lemon oil extracted from the peels of fresh lemons. Lemon oil is notable for its ability to improve digestion and nourish skin.

Organic Coconut

We use organic coconut - 75% fiber by composition - to add light sweetness and dietary fiber for ultimate digestibility of nutrients while keeping total carbs low.

Turmeric

Usually turmeric root contains 3-5% curcuminoids but ours is concentrated to 95%. This means better joints so you can get stronger everyday.

Maca

Maca is a highly nutritious superfood from Peru with tremendous antioxidant activity. It has a mild sweet butterscotch flavor that combines well with the lemon and coconut in our chocolate.
